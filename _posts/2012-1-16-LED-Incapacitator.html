Introduction

The purpose of this project was to explore the use of camera phones with flash as a distributed LED Incapacitator. While most camera phone flashes are only one color and lack the ability to modify their intensity, a closely clustered group of phones with strobing LED's should have a similar effect as an actual weapon. At the very least, single camera phone flashes should be able to take advantage of the Bucha Effect and so called Flicker Vertigo.

Results

The project is as of now unfinished. However I am specifically interested in measuring the disorientation caused by a camera phone flash strobing in the frequencies of 10-15 Hz, as well as different durations of the flash being active. If one phone or multiple phones working independently create a significant decrease in concentration and focusing, then a distributed app based on bluetooth synching of frequencies will be created.
