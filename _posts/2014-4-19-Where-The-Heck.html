Introduction

This project stemmed from a desire to figure out where to live in Seattle. I wanted a place with good access to grocery stores, restaurants, public transit, and cheap housing. Since all of this information is publically availible (ish...Lookin' at you craigslist), I thought I could aggregate all of this data into a heatmap. Also important was the ability to weigh things you find important, for example I care a lot about good transit and produce stands, but not at all about bars. This project can be viewed as a sort of reverse Walkscore, but which also selects and weighs businesses and transit more intelligently, as well as includes apartment cost data
